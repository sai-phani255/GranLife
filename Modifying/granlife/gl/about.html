About

Poised to be an international company and a world leader in the natural stone sector, GranLife already displays a potent growth, providing innovative, intelligent and inspiring leadership.
In a bid to promote quality granite and marble in the Indian and International market, this vibrant organization is into proactive trading and export consistently. At GranLife, the expertise reflects a deep experience in the natural stone industry for more than a decade now.
With this well-accomplished personnel, GranLife is ever expanding the horizon of innovative aesthetic solutions for the innumerable customers all over the world.
The company remains updated, and is aware of the contemporary customer-needs and preferences. Hence, as a facilitator of natural stones, GranLife ensures perennial delivery of bespoke quality products to the pertinent target audience.

Vision

Poise to become a prime supplier of natural stones with fresh ideas and innovations through an international supply chain that will offer value for money to all the customers. Being a dynamic company and open to changes, GranLife aims to be the first one on the contact list, whenever the needs for designer granite and marble arise.

Mission

To carve out a conspicuous niche for the company by consistently providing high quality, trustworthy materials and services. GranLife unrelentingly endeavors to satisfy customers through a perfect confluence of experience and creativity in pursuit of offering international industry standards.

Recognitions

GranLife is proudly certified by CAPEXIL, FIEO and Andhra Chamber of Commerce.
