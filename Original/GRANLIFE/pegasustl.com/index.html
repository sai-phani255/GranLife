Advantage Pegasus

By procuring natural stones from different geographical terrains, Pegasus enlarges their business horizons, and provides an expansive customer- choice. India being a vast reservoir of natural stone materials, each location has its own specialty with a mixture of fine nature and texture.

Not having a quarry or a factory has always been a distinct advantage for the company as it enables to bring quality and variety before our patrons, in a heterogeneous mix.

Learn More ...

Using the necessary infrastructure in and around the mines, Pegasus processes the quality of the stones only in 100% EOU factories and ensure that the overhead expenses for the customers are abated. Which implies that the target audience will always be paying only for the granite’s worth.

While it is true that the age of the stone makes its quality better, the company does not follow the ‘waiting-since-ages’ adage for our customers. In fact, the team at Pegasus operates 24/7 to provide the most fascinating materials to our customers sans any delay.
